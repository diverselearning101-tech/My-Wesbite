About Diverse Learning

Diverse Learning is dedicated to helping students of all ages succeed academically. Founded by Manny, who holds a Bachelor’s degree in Biochemistry and a Master’s degree in Computer Engineering, his approach combines deep subject knowledge with personalized teaching strategies. He teaches students from Grade 1 all the way to university level, tailoring lessons to individual learning styles and goals. He provides support for assignments, homework, and exam preparation in both online and in-person sessions (held at a local library).

  • Recognizes that students have unique learning styles and adapts teaching methods accordingly.
  • Combines visual, auditory, and hands-on learning strategies for better understanding.
  • Focuses on building long-term academic confidence and skills.
  • Supports both in-person and online learning environments.
